MoodCafe Disclaimer

The therapeutic relationship will only begin once the informed consent has been signed you, and following any advice given by the therapist is solely your discretion.

Online therapy using video and voice has been proven to be as effective as face to face session for most of the conditions and is considered to be the most effective interface for online therapy. Same may not be said about chat or email even though their efficacy has been seen in helping clients stay in therapy for long or resolving not too intense issues. It is agreed and acknowledged that online and one-to-one counselling /consulting are different in many respects and that outcomes may not be the same, and that any advice and direction given in no way would constitute an agreement or liability on the part of the provider.

We assure you of the best possible efforts from our therapists/counselors for  those who undergo the online therapy; however, please note that we do not  claim to cure each and every case, nor do we boast of an instant magical cure . We do not suggest that our online therapy is a replacement for the traditional models ways of providing therapy but research does prove the efficacy of video based online therapy as similar to face to face therapy. It is considered that the user has read and agreed to this disclaimer when using the information on this site, in the live chat, the daily task planners , the forum etc.
